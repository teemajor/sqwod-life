## EN

### Subject (pick one)
- Recovery just became a line item
- Your members are about to ask about cold plunges
- The recovery upsell your studio isn't selling yet

### Intro / preheader
The Global Wellness Institute just made contrast therapy official — which means your
members will ask about it before your P&L does.

### Connect-the-Dots — "Recovery is the upsell hiding in plain sight"
When an industry body like the GWI names contrast hydrotherapy a headline trend, it's
not a spa story — it's a demand signal. Member curiosity about cold plunge and sauna
becomes member expectation, and expectation becomes something people will pay extra for.
Rhone doubling down on physical stores and Fitness On Demand buying in boutique content
point the same way: the money is moving back to the in-person floor and the minutes
*around* the workout.

For an operator, that's a revenue line you can stand up without rebuilding your space —
a contrast/recovery add-on, a bookable 20-minute slot, a small monthly tier. Retention
lives in the gaps between classes; recovery is how you fill them.

### Do this
Stand up one bookable recovery service this quarter (contrast, sauna, or guided mobility)
and price it as a paid upgrade, not a freebie — test it with your most-engaged 20% first.

sondern ein Nachfragesignal. Wenn ein Branchenverband Eisbad und Sauna adelt, werden aus

wandert zurück auf die Fläche und in die Minuten rund ums Training.

Für Betreiber heißt das: eine Umsatzzeile, die du ohne Umbau aufmachst — ein
Regenerations-Add-on, ein buchbarer 20-Minuten-Slot, eine kleine Monatsstufe. Retention
entsteht in den Lücken zwischen den Kursen; Regeneration füllt sie.

### Do this / Mach das
Starte dieses Quartal ein buchbares Regenerationsangebot (Kontrast, Sauna oder geführte
Mobility) und bepreise es als bezahltes Upgrade, nicht als Gratis-Zugabe — teste es
zuerst mit deinen aktivsten 20 %.
