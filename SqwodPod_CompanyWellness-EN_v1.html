Questions

How much space does a Sqwod Pod need?

A Pod fits comfortably in roughly 25–40 m² of unused office, basement, or ground-floor space. If you have a vacant room, you likely have room for a Pod.

Who manages the gym and the wellness program?

Sqwod AI runs it end-to-end — booking, access, personalized programming, engagement nudges, and an anonymized usage dashboard for HR. No staffing or facilities burden on your team.

Is this a benefit for employees or a cost for employers?

Both win. Employees get a private, on-site training space and a guided program; employers get a differentiated benefit, better retention, and lower absenteeism — using space they already pay for.
