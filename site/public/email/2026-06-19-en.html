The gym is leaving the building
Three stories, one thread. Vibefam raised $1M to make booking software think; Athlean-X turned an audience into the whole business; a luxury marina is bolting a gym onto its docks. None of these is really about a room with weights in it.
The value is migrating off the floor — into software, audiences, and amenities. For a coach or studio owner that's uncomfortable but freeing: your four walls are your least defensible asset. Your data, your audience, and the relationship you own are what compound. Spend your next euro accordingly.
