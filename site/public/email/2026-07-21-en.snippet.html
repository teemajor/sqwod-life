DSSV's 2026 benchmarks: your new pricing north star
Every year operators set membership prices by vibes, competitor stalking, and mild panic. The DSSV's 2026 DACH benchmarks change that game — they give German-speaking gym and studio owners actual industry-wide figures to anchor pricing, gauge retention health, and spot where their unit economics are drifting off course. If your monthly fee feels 'about right,' these numbers will confirm it or embarrass you.
The concrete move: pull your current revenue-per-member, your cost-per-visit, and your membership tier mix, then stack them against the DSSV figures. Where you're above benchmark, you've got a story to tell investors or landlords. Where you're below, you've got a pricing conversation to have with yourself — before a competitor who read the same report has it for you.
