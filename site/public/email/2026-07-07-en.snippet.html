✓ Steal: Run a 'bring a friend free for a week' window tied to an existing challenge or milestone — referrals spike when there's a natural social moment to attach them to, not just an open invitation.
✓ Track: Start measuring Net Promoter Score monthly with a single post-class text: 'How likely are you to recommend us to a friend, 0–10?' Trends in that number predict churn and word-of-mouth growth before you see either on your P&L.
✓ Try: Create one member-only touchpoint that has nothing to do with training — a monthly coffee, a Spotify playlist they help curate, a members-only Q&A — something that builds identity around belonging, not just workouts.
