Ritual's Essential for Women 18+ is the multivitamin that made transparency the pitch. It's USP Verified and Clean Label Project tested — real third-party assurance that's rare in supplements — with full "Made Traceable" supplier disclosure, methylated B12 and folate, and a delayed-release capsule. It's vegan, non-GMO, and rates strongly across ~2,900 crowd reviews (4.5–4.6★). For a coach recommending a clean daily default to general clients, the quality signals are genuinely good.

The design choice to flag is the formula itself: it's deliberately minimalist — nine nutrients, no vitamin C, calcium, or most B vitamins — on the argument that most women already get those from diet. That makes it a "fill-the-gaps" supplement, not a complete multi. Add a subscription-first ~$33/month price, and note it doesn't ship to Germany or the EU directly (US/Canada/UK only), so DE readers need a forwarding service. Clean and trustworthy; just know what it deliberately leaves out.
