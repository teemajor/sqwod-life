Gymshark's Vital Seamless 2.0 is the brand's longest-running bestseller, and it earns the spot: squat-proof seamless knit, a clean silhouette, and a mid-market price (around £45 / $54) that undercuts the premium studio labels. For a coach recommending everyday kit to clients, it's the easy, recognizable pick — and Gymshark ships to Germany fast and cheap (€4.40 standard, free over €55) with a genuinely good shopping app behind it.

The honest caveat is service, not product. Independent platforms skew low — Reviews.io sits at 1.7★ and Trustpilot only "average" at 3.6★ — driven by returns and customer-service complaints, and being DTC-only means every exchange routes through Gymshark alone. The garment rates far better than the service does; just size with the snug seamless fit in mind. (Confirm the live EUR price on the German store before you buy.)
