Alo's High-Waist Airlift is the legging people mean when they say "Alo": a compressive, double-layered waistband, a sculpting fit, and a durable double-lined fabric with the subtle sheen that reads studio-to-street. Independent hands-on testing puts it at 4.2/5, and it runs true to size — for clients who want a premium look that holds up, it's the anchor piece in the range.

Two things temper it. It's a premium buy ($128–$134) with no pockets and only moderate breathability, so it's not the hot-yoga pick. And the brand's service reputation runs low on independent platforms — worth flagging separately from the product itself. For Germany, Alo runs a localized en-de store (confirm the live EUR price there), though direct orders still carry duties and a transatlantic returns path.
