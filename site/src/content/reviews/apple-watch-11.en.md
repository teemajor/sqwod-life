The Apple Watch Series 11 succeeds the Series 9 (with the Series 10 in between) and its headline win is the one owners actually feel: battery life rated up to 24 hours, plus new hypertension notifications, a Sleep Score, a brighter and more durable display, and a jump from LTE to 5G. It remains the best all-round smartwatch — no subscription, deep app ecosystem, ECG and now blood-pressure signals.

The catch is that it's an iterative upgrade. The design is largely unchanged from the Series 10, and most of the software wins arrive on older watches through watchOS 26 — so if you own a Series 9 or 10, there's little reason to move. That's why the value case, not the hardware, is what holds it just short of the top.
