The wearables market crosses €84bn in 2026, growing roughly 9% a year. But the headline number hides the real move: smart rings are up 31% year over year, pulling recovery and sleep data out of the enthusiast niche and into the mainstream.

For coaches and operators, that shift matters more than the topline. When your clients already wear continuous recovery tracking, the conversation changes from "should you track?" to "what do we do with the data?" — which is exactly where good coaching earns its keep.
